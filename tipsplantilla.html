Proper Chair Position

Proper chair positioning is crucial for maintaining good posture throughout your workday. Here are the key points to consider:

  • Adjust your chair height so your feet rest flat on the floor
  • Maintain a 90-degree angle at your knees
  • Keep your back straight and supported
